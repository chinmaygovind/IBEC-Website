Overview of the Competition

Each semester, IBEC teams take part in our exciting Regional Portfolio Competition. Every team starts with $100,000 in simulated currency and invests in a dynamic, competitive market environment. The region with the highest-valued portfolio by April 22nd wins the ultimate bragging rights... and maybe a little trophy.
