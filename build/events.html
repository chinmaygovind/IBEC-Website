Application Process

Application Period: August 29th - September 12th at 11:59 PM
Submit: Online application portal

Applications will be available after our information session. We're looking for motivated students passionate about international business and economics.

What We're Looking For

  • Interest in global business and economics
  • Curiosity about international markets and current events
  • Commitment to active participation in committee work
  • Collaborative mindset and willingness to learn
  • Academic standing in good order
