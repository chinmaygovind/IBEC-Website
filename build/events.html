§ 03 — Weekly

Every week.

Beyond the headline events, WIBEC runs a tight weekly cadence that keeps every member engaged with active markets.

MONDAY

Research roundtable.

Each committee shares one macro development from their region and its potential market implications. Open to all members.

THURSDAY

Investment committee.

Formal pitch sessions with Q&A. Officers vote on initiations, additions, and exits. Minutes posted to internal archive within 24 hours.

AS THEY COME

External speakers.

Faculty, practitioners, and alumni. Sessions are announced via the mailing list at least one week in advance.
