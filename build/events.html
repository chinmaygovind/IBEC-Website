New Member Welcome

Date: TBD (Late September)

Welcome to IBEC! New members will be introduced to their regional committees, learn about our student-managed fund, and begin their journey in global business analysis.

What We're Looking For

  • Interest in global business and economics
  • Curiosity about international markets and current events
  • Commitment to active participation in committee work

  • Academic standing in good order
